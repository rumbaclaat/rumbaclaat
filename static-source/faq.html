Orders & shipping

Standard UK delivery is 3–5 working days. Express options are available at checkout. Free shipping on orders over £50.

We currently ship throughout the UK and EU. International orders outside the EU are handled case-by-case via our trade team — contact us.

Order by Saturday 13 December for guaranteed UK delivery before the 25th. Express options extend this by a few days.

You'll receive a tracking link by email once your order ships. You can also find tracking in your account under Order History.

Contact hello@rumbaclaat.com within 1 hour of placing the order and we'll do our best. After dispatch, orders can't be changed but you can return them.

Age verification

UK law requires us to confirm everyone buying alcohol is at least 18 years old. We verify at checkout and our couriers also verify on delivery.

Someone aged 18 or over must be at the address to sign. Our couriers may ask for ID. If no one suitable is home, they'll attempt redelivery or leave a card.

We may cancel and refund the order. You're welcome to contact us if you think there's an issue.

Returns & refunds

You have 14 days to cancel most orders under the UK Consumer Contracts Regulations. Items must be unopened, unused and in resaleable condition. Perishables and personalised items are excluded.

Email hello@rumbaclaat.com with your order number and we'll send return instructions. You pay return postage unless the item is faulty.

Refunds are issued within 14 days of us receiving the return, to your original payment method.

We're sorry. Email us within 48 hours with a photo of the damage and we'll arrange a replacement or refund — no need to return the damaged item.

The rum

Our Original Reserve is distilled in Jamaica and aged in American oak. Each expression comes from selected Caribbean distilleries — see each product page for origin details.

Original Reserve and Spiced Gold are 700ml; ABV varies by expression (43% Original Reserve, 40% Spiced Gold). Check the product page for the specific bottle.

Our rums contain no allergens listed under UK FSA rules and are suitable for vegetarians. Spiced expressions may contain natural botanicals — see the product page ingredients.

Upright, sealed, away from direct sunlight. An opened bottle keeps its character for around 6–12 months.
